# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Church members (CRCKC 禮中堂) reading the Bible together as a community. Daily personal devotion context — a short focused session to read a chapter, mark it done, and feel connected to others doing the same. Mobile-first, since most users read on phones.

## Product Purpose

A distraction-free Bible reading tracker for the church community. Members read chapters, check them off, see who else is reading alongside them. The app exists to make daily Bible reading a shared, accountable practice — not just a private checkbox.

## Brand Personality

Clean, focused, elegant, reverent. The Bible text is the hero; the interface steps back to let scripture speak. Not flashy, not playful — dignified and calm, like a well-designed prayer book or a thoughtfully laid-out church bulletin. Dark mode as default supports night reading and a contemplative mood.

## Anti-references

- Over-designed "modern church" aesthetics (neon, heavy gradients, too much energy)
- Gamified UIs (badges, streaks, leaderboards)
- SaaS-style dashboards with charts and widgets
- Anything that competes with the scripture text for attention

## Design Principles

1. **The text is the star.** The Bible content should occupy 80%+ of the reading view. Every UI element is measured by whether it helps or hinders reading.
2. **One tap, one purpose.** The reading flow is linear: open → read → check → done. No modal surprise, no multi-step checkout, no extra friction between the reader and the chapter.
3. **Quiet competence.** Transitions are fast (150-200ms), not choreographed. The app loads into its task. Dark and calm, not trendy.
4. **Connected without being social.** Show the reader they're not alone (people-already-read counts, shared schedules) without turning Bible reading into a feed or a competition.
5. **Works offline, always.** The Bible is something you read anywhere — subway, mountainside, bedside. The PWA must serve content from local storage without a network request.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- Dark mode default with sufficient contrast for low-light reading
- Text zoom preference persisted locally
- Chinese text rendering at comfortable sizes (16px base, scalable)
- Reduced motion respected — no decorative animations
- WCAG AA minimum for all text and interactive elements
